How do I become a talent manager?

If you think you’re a great fit, send us an email at talentmanager@corefans.co to begin the application process

Who can become a talent manager?

Anyone who is passionate about helping creators grow and make money. Like us, you must be obsessed with getting creators the resources yhey need to keep creating. You must also have a great eye - the kind that recognizes a diamond in the rough.

How many Talents can I manage simultaenously?

Talent Managers cannot manage more than 50 talents at a given time. We don’t want our managers to get overwhelmed with the number of talents they manage. To make things easier, you get a dashboard where you can easily monitor all the talent you have recruited
